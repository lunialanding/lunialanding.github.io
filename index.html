Fashion Assistant for eCommerce

Lunia is for people who shop clothes over online and wants to make an informed decision before buying. It helps them to find the right cloth at the right price and which will fit just right.

How it works

Step 1: Set Measurements

Lunia takes your body measurements data to cater the right apparels across multiple brands which will fit you just right. Our data-driven personalization approach will ensure that there is no more confusion about sizes when you are trying out new brands!

Step 2: Search or Explore

Lunia lets you search products through conversation or through a visual search based on images - whether it is a photo you have taken, a screenshot or a picture from a magazine. You also have the option to simply explore a gallery containing thousands of products.

Step 3: Compare across brands and Checkout

Lunia allows you to compare prices, designs and reviews on the quality of visually similar products across multiple brands. It also allows you to check which brands have apparels that fit you best.
